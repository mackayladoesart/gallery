Bio

MacKayla is a part time illustrator currently working in Pittsburgh, PA. She has been drawing and painting for as long as she can remember, but she never planned to become a professional artist. In 2015 she became an undergraduate student double majoring in Marketing and Information Systems. Later that year, she felt inspired enough to create her YouTube Channel. She continued practicing and filming her process as a hobby while she studied and worked part time.

In 2017, MacKayla took the leap to participate in a local art event. This is where she sold her first piece. After this, MacKayla decided to pursue art further. She opened her first online store through Society6 in early 2018. Later that same year, she earned her bachelor's degree, designed her website, and opened her second online store through storenvy. She is building her online portfolio and shop, and is reaching out to participate in more local art events.

MacKayla currently works with a large range of mediums, though she is looking to focus more on digital work. Generally, she loves fantasy themes, but her favorite subjects include nature and animals. She also loves playing with color theory and incorporating it into her work.
