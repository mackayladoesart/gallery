About MacKayla

Did you go to art school?

I did not get a degree related to art, but I did double major in Marketing and Information Systems. I feel that a business degree has really helped me with aspects of an art career I would have never realized otherwise. I have also continuously taken art classes throughout highschool and college. These classes were important to me because not only did they teach me new things, but also made sure I was consistently practicing and working.

Aspiration

How do I get started?

That's really the question, but you can start anywhere. I recommend looking at a bunch of different artists, and following their YouTube channels and instagrams. See their techniques, it will give you ideas. Also, just practice everyday. More importantly, do not give up or get down about yourself. We all get stuck somewhere, the difference is if you choose to push on or not.

Art Techniques

What materials do you use?

I actually use a lot of materials, ranging from watercolors, acrylic, oils, pastels, charcoal, colored pencils, copic markers, and digital. I also do not really consider myself brand loyal yet. I am currently trying to dwelve deeper into digital art, which is probably one of my weakest areas.

Production

Do you make your own prints from home?

I do not at the current moment. A goal of mine is to get to that point one day, but as of right now it is worth it to me to go an outside source to save the time to print. I currently use CatPrint for prints, VistaPrint for business cards, and StickerMule for stickers. Feel free to check out my referall codes page if you want to get discounts from them.

Business

How do you gain followers?

Interacting consistently with your social media is the best way to gain an audience. Many platforms have algorithms now that only initially show your posts to a certain percentage of your followers. If your post gets enough interaction, then it will be shown to a larger percentage of your followers and so on. Encouraging interaction is the best option. Also, interacting with your followers or other artists will generally just make you more visible. I am a shy and unsocial person, but once I got the courage to start interacting more I saw great improvement.

What social media platforms do you use?

You can find all links to my social media in the footer. However, I mainly use Instagram. It is the site that gets me the most interactions, and I just generally have more fun and enjoy using it.
